Dr. Henry De Malmanche

Postdoctoral Researcher

Dr Henry de Malmanche is a molecular biologist and biotechnologist working at the intersection of insect cell culture, virology, and vaccine manufacturing. His research focuses on the use of insect cell culture for the production of insect viruses. His current work is centred around developing mosquito cell manufacturing bioprocess to underscore the ISVac platform to enable scalable production of next-generation vaccines and diagnostics for mosquito-borne disease. He brings a strong translational perspective, with experience spanning virus engineering, upstream and downstream bioprocessing, and technology transfer. His work aims to bridge fundamental virology with real-world manufacturing and outbreak-response capability.
